Cartesian Cover Assembly

1.1 Cartesian Cover Assembly
Go to www.febtop.com/support, download and print 10 pieces of Magnet Holder. Place the magnet in the lower square
attached with double sided tape.

Use screws to mount them on an acrylic plate (both sides).

Mount the side panels to the Cartesian. Fix the front and the back of the acrylic plate, then install the hinges on two
cover acrylic panels.
